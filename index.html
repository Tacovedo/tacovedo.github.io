What We Do

Tacovedo Toneworks is a small independent studio business focused on capturing, shaping, and supporting great sound. We operate as a recording studio, a source for used musical instruments and gear, and a home for studio-related software and tools we design and build.

Our work spans session recording, tone shaping, and project support for artists, makers, and collaborators, alongside carefully selected second-hand instruments and studio equipment, and custom utilities for modern audio workflows.
